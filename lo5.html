LO5 - Personal Leadership

Self-aware growth, values-driven actions, and learning attitude

You are aware of your strengths and weaknesses in ICT and personal development. You choose actions aligned with your core values to promote personal growth and a learning attitude. The portfolio itself proves this and your other learning outcomes through your own authentic form and system (website, magazine, showreels, etc.).

Self-Awareness

Reflect on ICT skills, tools, communication, and mindset; identify strengths and development areas honestly.

Value-Based Actions

Link decisions to values (e.g., curiosity, integrity, sustainability) and show how actions reflect them.

Learning Attitude

Document continuous learning: experiments, courses, feedback use, reflection notes, and improvement plans.

Personal Leadership Process

1
🪞
Strength & Weakness Log

Maintain a self-assessment record with achievements and improvement goals.

Example: Strengths - problem solving, collaborative coding; Weaknesses - stakeholder presentation, frontend animation polish.
2
🎯
Values-Driven Goals

Set action plans based on personal values and track progress.

Example: Value=Quality, Action=build end-to-end test plan. Value=Learning, Action=weekly skill journal and peer review.
3
📚
Portfolio proof setup

Choose technical system that reflects your essence (custom website, magazine, showreel plus docs).

Example: This portfolio is an expressive website with dark-futuristic theme, using interactive case studies to show process transparency.
4
📈
Learning journal & reflection

Log feedback, iterations, challenges, and insights played back into development cycles.

Example: Reflect on sprint review: “Need stronger tests; invested 2h studying jest; next deliverable includes tests”.
