LO4 - Professional Standard

Professional methodology, stakeholder involvement and ethical responsibility

Both individually and in teams, you apply a relevant methodological approach from the professional field to set project goals, involve stakeholders, conduct applied research, give advice, decide, and deliver reports. This includes logging ethical, intercultural and sustainability factors throughout the process.

Methodological Approach

Use established methodologies (e.g., Agile, SRD, human-centered design, research-based frameworks) to form project goals and plan execution to industry standards.

Stakeholder Involvement

Engage stakeholders with clear communication, maintain feedback loops, and align decision-making with client expectations and project scope.

Ethical & Sustainable Practices

Keep ethics, intercultural sensitivity, and sustainability visible in design choices, research, and final recommendations.

Key Professional Activities

1
🧾
Project Goal Definition

Express measurable and realistic goals that reflect project purpose, user needs, and stakeholder priorities.

Example goal: Improve onboarding conversion by 25% in 3 months through iterative usability improvements and stakeholder alignment.
2
🤝
Stakeholder Collaboration

Document meetings, requirements and decisions with reports and tools like stakeholder maps, approval logs and communication plans.

Example: Weekly sprint reviews, process checkpoints, and stakeholder sign-offs for major scope changes and risk mitigation.
3
🔬
Applied Research

Conduct user studies, market surveys, feasibility scans, and technical research—reporting methods and findings clearly.

Example: Combine qualitative interviews with quantitative analytics to support recommendation decisions in a coherent presentation.
4
✅
Decision & Reporting

Provide advisories and decision documentation, including trade-offs, risk implications, and recommended next steps with traceable logic.

Example: Present 3 options, cost/benefit, and final target path with supporting justification for stakeholders.

Professional Standard Showcase

Ethics & Sustainability Tracking

Track ethical criteria (accessibility, bias, privacy) and sustainability metrics (energy use, material choice, long-term impact) in project logs.

Ethical Framework

Intercultural Engagement

Include diverse audience perspectives in research and design evaluation to ensure culturally aware and inclusive product outcomes.

Inclusion

Final Delivery Pack

Deliver a consolidated report including project background, outcomes, strategy, process, decisions, and future recommendations to stakeholders.
