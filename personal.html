Core Idea

n a world where time has been frozen to prevent catastrophe, a young Weaver sets out to uncover the truth behind a system that may be holding everything together… or holding it back.

Theme

Control vs change, and the cost of preserving a world at the expense of its future.

Direction

I'm focusing on creating the entire narrative first and concept first, with this my friends can get started on 3d modeling and drawing once the world and characters are more fleshed out. I want the story to be the foundation that the gameplay and art are built around, so I'm prioritizing it in development.
